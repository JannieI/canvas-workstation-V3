Palette Button Bar
Help

Manage your palette button bar (displayed when you are in EditMode to change Widgets). Each menu option is represented by a button, and the buttons are colour coded per main menu option. The list on the left hand are all available buttons, while the right hand list shows the ones that have been selected. Use the arrows to change your selected buttons. Click the reset button to restore the original selection of buttons (which you can then modify afterwards). Click the X or press Escape to close this form.
